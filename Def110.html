About Defender 110

The Land Rover Defender 110 is a mid-size luxury 5-door SUV that combines rugged off-road capability with premium comforts and modern tech — ideal for families who want adventure without sacrificing refinement.

Key Features

  • Engine Optional
  • 2.0 L turbo petrol (≈296 bhp & ~400 Nm)
  • 3.0 L diesel mild-hybrid (~296 bhp & ~650 Nm)
  • Transmission: 8-speed automatic with AWD.
  • Seating: Typically up to 7 seats (varies by trim)
  • Off-Road Capability: Terrain Response, high ground clearance, robust suspension systems
  • Interior: Premium infotainment, luxury seating, modern safety and driver-assist features

Performance

  • Powerful AWD drivetrain designed to tackle rough terrain and smooth highways with ease.

Price

  • Approx. Range (India): ₹98 Lakh - ₹1.70 Cr+ depending on engine/trim/options.

    • Note: Prices vary significantly by city, variant, GST changes, and optional features.
