About Defender 130

The Land Rover Defender 130 is a full-size luxury SUV with an extended body and up to 8 seats, making it perfect for large families or those who need max space without compromising on off-road capability or comfort.

Key Features

  • Engine Optional
  • 3.0 L diesel mild-hybrid (~300 bhp & ~650 Nm)
  • Petrol variants and larger engines available in broader Defender lineup
  • Transmission: 8-speed automatic with AWD
  • Seating & Space: Up to 8 passengers with ample cargo space
  • Capability: Same rugged off-road DNA, terrain systems, high load capacity
  • Dimensions: Longer body vs Defender 110 for more interior room.

Performance

  • Engine and drivetrain tuned for a balance of power, stability, and comfort — strong diesel/hybrid performance for heavy loads and long journeys..

Price

  • Approx. Range (India): ₹1.39 Cr - ₹1.63 Cr+ depending on variant and features.

    • Note: Prices vary significantly by city, variant, GST changes, and optional features.
