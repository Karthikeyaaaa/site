About Defender 90

The Land Rover Defender 90 is a compact luxury off-road SUV that blends rugged capability with premium comfort, designed to take on tough terrain while still being refined for everyday driving.

Key Features

  • Engine Options: Available with powerful petrol and diesel engines — including a supercharged 5.0 L V8 that produces around 518 bhp and 625 Nm torque.
  • Automatic Transmission: 8-speed automatic gearbox with AWD for excellent all-surface capability.
  • Off-Road & Comfort: Strong off-road geometry with high ground clearance; premium interior with features like panoramic roof, Meridian sound system, heated/cooled seats and advanced driver aids (ADAS).
  • Seating: Typically 5 seats (some trims offer a front jump seat for a 6th passenger).

Performance

  • Power: ~518 bhp
  • Torque: ~625 Nm
  • 0-100 km/h: ~5.2 s
  • Top Speed: ~240 km/h
  • Drivetrain: All-Wheel Drive (AWD)

Price

  • Approx. Range: ~₹1.25 Cr – ₹1.42 Cr (ex-showroom India) depending on variant and engine
  • Diesel variants are generally at the lower end of this range.
  • The 5.0 L V8 petrol Defender 90 sits toward the higher end.

    • Note: Prices vary significantly by city, variant, GST changes, and optional features.
